command to update grub in fedora:-
sudo grub2-mkconfig -o /boot/efi/EFI/fedora/grub.cfg

or 

use this:-
sudo grub2-mkconfig -o /boot/grub2/grub.cfg


command to create initramfs in fedora:-
dracut --kver [version of kernerl] -f(force) [name of initramfs]

listing the contents of initramfs:-
lsinitrd -v [name]
